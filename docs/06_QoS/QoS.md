# CLOUD-LINK NETWORKS  
## Quality of Service (QoS) Documentation

---

## 1.0 Overview
This document explains the **Quality of Service (QoS)** configuration in the CLOUD-LINK NETWORKS ISP enterprise network. QoS ensures that **critical traffic receives priority** while maintaining efficient use of network resources.

---

## 2.0 QoS Objectives
- Prioritize critical traffic such as:
  - VoIP and video conferencing  
  - Business applications  
  - Management and monitoring traffic
- Limit bandwidth for non-critical traffic (e.g., bulk downloads, streaming)
