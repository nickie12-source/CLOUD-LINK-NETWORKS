## 3.0 Company Overview
CLOUD-LINK NETWORKS is a modern Internet Service Provider (ISP) that provides high-speed internet connectivity to both residential and business customers. The company operates an enterprise-level network infrastructure that connects directly to the internet backbone (cloud) and distributes services through a well-structured and secure network topology.

The company focuses on providing:

- Reliable internet connectivity
- High bandwidth performance
- Secure network services
- Affordable internet plans
- Scalable network infrastructure for future growth
