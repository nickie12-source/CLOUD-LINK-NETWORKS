# CLOUD-LINK NETWORKS  
## IP Addressing and VLANs Documentation

---

## 1.0 Overview
This document outlines the **IP addressing scheme** and **VLAN configuration** for the CLOUD-LINK NETWORKS ISP enterprise network. Proper IP allocation and VLAN segmentation ensure **security, performance, and ease of management**.

---

## 2.0 VLAN Design
Each department is assigned a unique VLAN to separate traffic and provide security boundaries.

| Department                     | VLAN ID | VLAN Name                 |
|--------------------------------|---------|--------------------------|
| Network Operations Center (NOC) | 10      | VLAN_NOC                 |
| Customer Support Department     | 20      | VLAN_CS                  |
| Finance Department              | 30      | VLAN_FINANCE             |
